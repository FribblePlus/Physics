Physical Quantities

Physical quantities are properties or attributes of matter that can be measured and quantified

Examples of physical quantities:

  • length
  • mass
  • time
  • temperature
  • electric charge

Physical quantities are typically measured using units, which provide a standard way of expressing the magnitude of the quantity.
